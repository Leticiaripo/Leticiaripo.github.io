Wandar

WANDAR is an AR application that allows users to create and view hyperlocal 3D drawings within designated hotspots in the world. The application aims to use the phone as a tool to bring people together in the present moment and collaborate within a new social platform.

The team felt not many platforms utilise smartphones in a way that brings people together. More often than not, smartphones are detrimental to social interactions, inhibiting meaningful connections with others.

WANDAR will make smartphones an integral part of a new type of social experience, changing them from being an inhibitor of social gatherings into a facilitator of bringing people together in the moment.

Smartphones have become an extension of ourselves and are a central part of our daily lives. WANDAR wants to utilise this dependency to develop a social experience where being on your smartphone is part of the experience. Using AR, WANDAR grounds these experiences in the real-world, bringing people together in a physical location.

To ensure that WANDAR is easily accessible to all types of users, the act of creating drawings was chosen as it is an activity that anybody can engage and participate in. Branding Guidelines
