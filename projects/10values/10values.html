Ten Typographic Values
of a Modern Family

10 T.V.M.F is an Augmented Reality experience with the intention to better understand and implement storytelling practices into a digital artefact that tells my story through a self-initiated journey.

In a digital attempt to bring my family members closer to Vancouver, 10 T.V.M.F is a visualization of the words and childhood values taught to me by the closest of my relatives marrying typography and augmented reality.

10 T.V.M.F is an Augmented Reality experience made during my time at the CDM with the intention to better understand and implement storytelling practices into a digital artefact that tells my story through semiotics, semantics, and pragmatics that speak to me personally.

The positioning of the text around the viewer is intended to present a petal storytelling structure as it has been used in traditional forms of media in the past and applied in a contemporary version of digital media. This allows the viewer to explore the different blocks of text more freely, being able to change their sight from one to another without correlation or an intended order perhaps identifying certain patterns or words within the blocks that change their perception of the piece (a reached conclusion).
