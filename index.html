For teams looking to increase their diversity of viewpoints through different lenses in creative pipelines I offer design sensibilities, framework development, and a unique hyperbolic empathy to spark and materialize ideas through carefully crafted custom solutions that will help connect with your user and help grow your business.

I’m a multidisciplinary designer from Spain who grew up surrounded by third culture kids with a variety of influences and points of view. My international education broadened my understanding of different cultures and it has made me into the endlessly curious person I am today. This curiosity then drove me to move to Berlin and London where I pursued my passion for design and expanded my horizon of the possibilities of creation and everything it entails.

I have worked globally with clients from different backgrounds and after having completed Foundation at Central Saint Martins College of Art and Design I decided to continue my studies at London College of Communications focusing on Graphic and Media Design.

During my time in London I also undertook a series of self-initiated projects as an attempt to translate physical mediums to digital experiences. It was at this point that my work shifted towards listening closely to the users’ needs while continuing to think strategically and designing bespoke solutions for complex problems.

My growing interest in cutting edge technologies and interactive experiences led me to the MDM program were I have been working on a series of mixed reality projects. At the CDM I was introduced to the ‘awe’ feeling that my design thinking is led by in an attempt to make people feel and be reminded of what it means to be human.
